# Types of AI Cover Art Tools

Indie artists typically group AI cover art services into two categories:

## General Image Generators
Examples people talk about:
- Fotor
- NightCafe
- OpenArt
- Pixelcut
- BasedLabs

These are broad text-to-image tools that happen to support an album-cover preset or template. The underlying model is trained for general image generation, not specifically for music artwork.

## Music-Trained Cover Art Tools
Examples people talk about:
- CoverArtGenerator.ai
- Artificial Studio
- Daft Art

These are built around the album cover use case: square output by default, prompts tuned to genre and mood, attention to title/artist text rendering, and commercial licensing that assumes the output will be uploaded to streaming services.

Artists often try both types and pick based on whether they need legible in-image text or a more open-ended visual experiment.
